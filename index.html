About Us

Welcome to 6 Minutes of Solitude.

We’re a group of people with a mindset-
that the world today suffers from a distinct lack of solitude.

A lack of moments to breathe,
moments of introspection,
moments where we can reflect on where we are
&
where we want to be.

We’re a group of people with a mission.
We want to make the world a calmer, richer, more soulful place.
And to do that we simply ask you a question.

Can you give 6 minutes?
6 minutes out of your day- every day.
In your 24 hours,
in your 1440 minutes.

Can you give 6 minutes a day to find your happy place.
To do whatever it is that makes you happy.
To breathe, to reflect,
to appreciate the beautiful, beautiful gift of life.

To find your solitude.
Whatever it may be, wherever it may take you-

find your solitude.
